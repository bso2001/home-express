===============================

taken from the album "Foxtrot" by Genesis.

This is my attempt of transcribing the electric guitar parts at the
end of the song.

- the < at the beginning means a fast fade-in with a volume pedal
- the finger settings in the 2nd and 3rd line are my 'personal note'
  I exercised it that way but there surely are better ways for this part

Any suggestions and corrections are highly welcome,

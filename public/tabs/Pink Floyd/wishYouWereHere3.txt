Here's the deal.  Personaly this is one of my most favorite songs
play to help me relax or just something to do.  I have seen a lot 
tabs on the internet for this song, but they all seem to be just
a little bit different from each other and they dont quite fit into
the song right.  So what I have done is spent many hours working on
putting all the peices to the song together so that they all fit.
So this should be the closest version, if not exactly how Pink
Floyd plays it, that you will ever hear.

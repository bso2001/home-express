OK!  I have finally unlocked the mystery of the acoustic intro to this 
song!  It is tuned down a WHOLE step and played in E minor, so it sounds 
like D minor.  The real way David Gilmour played the four intro chords is 
like this:

   Em9  Cadd4 Bsus2  Bbsus2
1---2-----0-----0-----0--
2---3-----1-----2-----1--
3---0-----0-----4-----3--
4---2-----3-----4-----3--
5---------3-----2-----1--

                              A PILLOW OF WINDS
                               (Waters/Gilmour)
                                by Pink Floyd
                         from the album "Meddle" (1971)
                              
Tabbed by: Ernesto
tamariscos2002@yahoo.com.ar

I was surprised not to find a tab of this beautiful song on the net 
(just chords but in a not very accurate version) so I decided to post it
like I play it on this standard tuning simplyfied version (there are
more guitars than this on the record, including two diferent ones for 
the slide fills). Timing and vibrato are the hardest parts to get 
the slide sound, but with a little practice I think this will be good 
enought to get started. Here goes the slide fills and the chords.
Sorry for my english I'm from Argentina.

                  ______________Chords_____________

Chords must be played repeating this pattern all through the song:
                
e-0-------------0----
B----------0---------
G-------0------------
D----0--------0------
A-0-------------0----
E---------0----------
                (Start again here)

It's the same pattern used for Beatles songs "Julia", 
"Dear Prudence", "Octopus Garden" and the beguining of
"Happiness is a warm gun" and John learned it from folk singer Donovan.

When added notes are indicated for chords (E/F# for example)
these must be played on 5th and 6th strings.

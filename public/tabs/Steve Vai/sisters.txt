|-----PM---|  - Palm Muting

5p3    - Pull off
3/(5)  - Bend
(5)\3  - Release Bend
2/4    - Slide
<7>    - Natural Harmonic
<5.17> - Tap harmonic - Fret at 5th fret, tap 17th fret quickly

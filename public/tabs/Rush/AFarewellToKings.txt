Rush - A Farewell to Kings

        From the CD 'A Farewell to Kings'
                Written by Lee/Lifeson/Peart
                Mercury/Polygram Records 822546-2

        This constitutes about 90% of the song. I re-worked the
introduction, but it is still almost the same as the original by David
Sandberg <dts@quad.sialis.com>. The entire middle section of the song is
missing in this tab. It was just too difficult for me to transcribe.
Additional credit goes to Chuck Bruce <cbruce@peco-energy.com> for figuring
out Riff 1 and Dave Toussaint <bigdave@pe.net> for various riffs. The intro
is in the key of D, then switches to A. It ends in D after the last set of
lyrics.

Jimmy Pena - jimmy@walrus.com

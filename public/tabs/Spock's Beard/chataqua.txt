           guitar down one step for some reason. But I kept on tabbing the
           whole tab as I had started it, and then simply changed all 8's to
           9's, all 7's to 8's, all 6's to ... well, you get the picture.
           So, therefore there are two versions of this tab in this file.
           The first one needs -1 tuning for album pitch, and must be the
           way mr. Morse plays it, the second needs standard tuning, for
           album pitch.

                                                            Cherio, Macahan.
